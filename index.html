About Us

The story of The Bargain Boys started in 2024, when Eddie Letsoalo (current CEO) and his business partner,
Thabo Cobra (current CFO), were among the first people to bring international franchising to the country.

The duo introduced South Africans to several franchises,
one of which was The Bargain Boys an Botswana franchise that focused exclusively on the buying and selling of quality pre-owned goods.

The Bargain Boys continues to hold the master franchise licence in sub-Saharan Africa; we have stores in South Africa,
Namibia and Nigeria, with plans to expand further across the African continent.
